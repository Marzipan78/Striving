Simple Sandwich

How to make a sandwich

Ingredients

  1. Regular-ass Bread
  2. Mayonaisse
  3. Cheese
  4. Pickle
  5. Tomato
  6. Onion
  7. Butter

Instructions

  1. Grab your regular-ass Bread and spread the Butter on one side and lay it on a hot pan
  2. Spread the Mayonaisse on the untoasted side of the Bread slice
  3. Layer your Cheese, Pickle, Tomato and Onion
  4. Season it with Salt & Pepper and just enjoy it!
